LIMPID is an innovative project aimed at analyzing the water quality of reservoirs impacted by various activities. By collecting data from different sensors, LIMPID creates comprehensive datasets that are used for in-depth analysis and monitoring of water conditions. Our goal is to ensure the safety and sustainability of water resources through advanced technology and data science.

Key Features

  • Real-time water quality monitoring
  • Comprehensive data collection and analysis
  • Integration with various sensors and devices
  • User-friendly dashboard for data visualization
  • Support for decision making in environmental management
